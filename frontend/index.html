About Our School

SKYSOAR International School is a premier educational institution committed to academic excellence and character development.

Founded in 2005, we have grown from a small neighborhood school to a leading educational institution with over 1,000 students across nursery, primary and secondary levels.

Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment where students can discover their passions, develop critical thinking skills, and grow into responsible global citizens.
